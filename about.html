At SoupGroup, we don't just see a bowl of soup – we see a bowl of hope. A simple serving can represent warmth, comfort, and community in challenging times. This simple meal is a reflection of our larger ideology: giving back to the community with open hearts.

Founded in 2023, our mission has always been crystal clear: to make nourishing meals accessible to everyone. By aggregating soup kitchens across various cities, we ensure no one goes to bed on an empty stomach. Irrespective of socio-economic status, we believe that everyone deserves a warm meal, especially when times are tough.

But SoupGroup is more than just a platform; it's a movement. A movement that bridges the gap between soup kitchens and the very people they aim to serve. It's a place for kitchens to increase their visibility and extend their reach to those who need them the most. And for volunteers? A way to find opportunities to make a tangible difference in their communities.

As we look towards the future, our goals remain steadfast. We aim to be the easiest and most accessible option for those in need. More than that, we want to be a beacon of hope and a testament to the power of community-driven service.

We believe that making a difference starts with a single step, or in our case, a single meal. So join us as we stir pots, pour ladles, and fill bowls with more than just soup – with love, hope, and solidarity.
